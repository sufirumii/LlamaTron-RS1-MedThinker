About GuideXcelra

The interdisciplinary learning platform built for the future of life sciences.

Our Vision

Breaking silos between biology and technology

Life sciences organizations face a critical challenge: their teams speak different scientific languages. GuideXcelra was created to bridge this gap — empowering every professional to understand and collaborate across domains.

🧬 Built for Excelra's Vision
What We Solve
  • Biotech professionals who can't leverage AI tools due to the knowledge gap
  • Data scientists who don't understand the biology behind the data they analyze
  • Slow onboarding for new hires joining interdisciplinary scientific teams
  • No centralized knowledge system for scientific terminology and SOPs
  • Lack of personalized learning paths for career transitions in life sciences
The Problem We Solve
🧱

Knowledge Silos

Biotech, data science, and engineering teams operate in isolation, creating miscommunication, slow projects, and missed opportunities for innovation.

📚

Generic Learning

Existing platforms like Coursera and LinkedIn Learning offer generic courses — not tailored to the specific needs of interdisciplinary life sciences teams.

🔍

Lost Knowledge

Organizational knowledge is scattered across emails, documents, and individual heads. When employees leave, critical domain expertise disappears with them.

Use Case at Excelra

Why GuideXcelra Matters for Excelra

Excelra operates at the intersection of pharmaceutical research, data science, and technology services. Teams across scientific data management, pharmacovigilance, bioinformatics, and software engineering need a shared vocabulary and accelerated skill acquisition. GuideXcelra serves as the AI-powered learning layer that unifies these teams — reducing onboarding time by 40%, improving cross-functional communication, and enabling faster delivery of scientific insights.

Cheminformatics Excelra Product

GOSTAR — Global Online Structure-Activity Relationship Database

GOSTAR is Excelra's flagship SAR database containing over 7.5 million compounds with experimentally validated bioactivity data, ADMET properties, and mechanistic annotations. It powers drug discovery workflows across target identification, lead optimisation, and safety profiling for medicinal chemists and computational scientists worldwide.

Bioinformatics Excelra Product

GoBiom — Genomics & Biomarker Intelligence Platform

GoBiom is Excelra's curated genomics and biomarker database integrating multi-omics data — genomic variants, gene expression, proteomics, and clinical outcomes — to accelerate biomarker discovery and translational research. It enables precision medicine teams to identify patient stratification markers and companion diagnostic candidates with deep literature traceability.

Scientific Informatics Excelra Workflow

GOSTAR + GoBiom Integration — Linked Chemistry-Biology Intelligence

Excelra's integrated GOSTAR–GoBiom workflow connects small-molecule SAR data with genomic and biomarker context, enabling end-to-end drug discovery intelligence from target biology through compound optimisation. This linked intelligence layer is a key differentiator for Excelra's CRO and data science clients seeking multi-dimensional insights in a single platform.

SOP / Workflow Excelra Process

Excelra Data Curation SOP — GOSTAR Compound Annotation Workflow

Standard Operating Procedure for Excelra's GOSTAR data curation process: covers primary literature extraction, structure standardisation using RDKit/ChemDraw, bioactivity annotation rules, ADMET flag assignment, and quality control checkpoints. This SOP is followed by Excelra's 300+ scientific curators across Hyderabad and global delivery centres to maintain GOSTAR's industry-leading data quality.
